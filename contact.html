Frequently Asked Questions

What are your current COVID-19 safety measures?

We follow all local health guidelines. Currently, masks are optional but recommended in indoor areas. Hand sanitizer stations are available throughout the park.

Can I bring outside food into the park?

Yes, you're welcome to bring your own food and non-alcoholic beverages. Picnic areas are available near the main entrance.

Do you offer wheelchair rentals?

Yes, we have a limited number of wheelchairs and ECVs available for rent on a first-come, first-served basis at the main entrance.

How can I support the park's conservation efforts?

You can support us by becoming a member, donating, or adopting an animal through our conservation program.
