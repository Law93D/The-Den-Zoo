Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to know before visiting Den-Doo Wildlife Park

Hours of Operation

Open daily (except December 25)

  • Spring/Summer (Apr-Sep): 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Fall/Winter (Oct-Mar): 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Last admission: 1 hour before closing

Special evening events may have different hours.

By Public Transport

Bus: Take route 42 to Wildlife Park stop

Train: Nearest station is Wildville Central (2 miles away)

Shuttle service available from train station weekends and holidays.

By Car

From downtown Wildville:

  1. Take Highway 7 west
  2. Exit at Conservation Way
  3. Follow signs to park entrance

Free parking for 500 vehicles. Overflow parking available on busy days.

Park Map

Accessibility

The Den Zoo Wildlife Park is committed to being accessible to all visitors:

  • Wheelchair accessible paths throughout 90% of the park
  • Motorized scooter rentals available
  • Accessible restrooms in all main areas
  • Braille guidebooks available at entrance
  • Sign language interpreters available with 2 weeks notice

Please contact us in advance for any special accommodation needs.
